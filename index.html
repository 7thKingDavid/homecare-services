Our Services

Read about the special services we offer

Our Companion Care services are designed to provide emotional support, companionship, and assistance with daily activities for your loved ones who may be feeling isolated or lonely. Our trained caregivers offer personalized care to enhance the quality of life for your family members, helping them to remain independent and engaged in their communities. Our goal is to enable your family members to live safely and comfortably in their own homes, enhancing their overall well-being and quality of life.

Transitioning from a hospital stay back to home can be a challenging time for both patients and their families. At Chrysoprase, our Hospital to Home care services are designed to ensure a smooth and safe transition, providing personalized care and support to help your loved ones regain their strength and independence. From medication management to mobility assistance, our caregivers are here to assist every step of the way, giving you peace of mind during this critical time.

We understand that everyone's care needs are unique. That's why we offer flexible Hourly Home Care services tailored to meet the specific needs and schedules of your family. Whether you require assistance for a few hours a day or around-the-clock care, our compassionate caregivers are available to provide personalized care and support. From personal care to light housekeeping, we are committed to helping your loved ones maintain their independence and quality of life in the comfort of their own homes.
